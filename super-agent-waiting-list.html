🎯 What Is Super Agent?

Super Agent is an advanced AI evaluator that performs a complete screening test on any AI agent — including LLMs, chatbots, and custom APIs — and delivers detailed performance analytics in a statistical format.

  • Evaluates LLMs, chatbots, and custom APIs
